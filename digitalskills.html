Frequently asked questions

Click the question below:

Q. What are the eligibility criteria for enrolling in the courses?

To enroll in our courses, different eligibility criteria apply based on the specific course:
  • For Java and Python courses, an interest in learning coding is required.
  • Software testing courses have no coding requirements.
  • Advanced Office, Tally, Power BI, Tableau courses require an interest in creativity.
  • For the Spoken French course, a basic knowledge of English is necessary.

Q. What mode of training is provided for the courses?

The courses are delivered through online and live hands-on training mode, providing participants with a dynamic and interactive learning experience.

Q. Will recordings of the lectures be provided?

Yes, recordings of the lectures will be made available to participants for reference and review.

Q. Is it possible to change the batch timing?

Yes, participants have the flexibility to change the batch timing to suit their schedule and preferences.

Q. If a lecture is missed, can it be revisited later?

Yes, participants have the opportunity to revisit and revise missed lectures to ensure a comprehensive understanding of the course material.

Q. What resources are provided, including notes, assignments, quiz, projects, and certifications?

Comprehensive resources, including notes, assignments, quizzes, projects, and certifications, are made available to participants to enhance their learning experience and skill development.

Q. Is placement assistance provided after completing the courses?

Yes, participants receive placement assistance to facilitate their entry into the workforce upon successful completion of the courses.

Q. Can participants suggest additional topics for inclusion in the courses?

Yes, participants are encouraged to suggest additional topics, especially if they are deemed important for interviews or further skill development.
