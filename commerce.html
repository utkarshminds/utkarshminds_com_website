  • Advanced Office, Tally, Power BI, Tableau courses require an interest in creativity.
  • For the Spoken French course, a basic knowledge of English is necessary.

Q. Can participants suggest additional topics for inclusion in the courses?

Basic to Advanced statistical data analysis

₹25000(90 hours)

  • Basic Level

    • Introduction to Statistics
      • Definition and importance of statistics.
      • Types of data: qualitative vs. quantitative.
      • Scales of measurement: nominal, ordinal, interval, and ratio.
    • Descriptive Statistics
      • Measures of central tendency: mean, median, and mode.
      • Measures of variability: range, variance, standard deviation, and interquartile range.
      • Data visualization: bar charts, histograms, pie charts, and box plots.
    • Probability
      • Basic concepts of probability.
      • Counting rules and sample spaces.
      • Conditional probability and the basics of Bayes' theorem.
      • Discrete and continuous probability distributions (uniform, binomial, normal).
    • Hypothesis Testing and Confidence Intervals
      • Introduction to hypothesis testing.
      • Type I and Type II errors.
      • Concept of a p-value.
      • Construction of confidence intervals.

  • Intermediate Level

    • Inferential Statistics
      • Z-tests, t-tests for one or two samples.
      • Analysis of Variance (ANOVA) for comparing more than two groups.
      • Chi-square tests for categorical data.
    • Correlation and Regression
      • Correlation coefficient interpretation.
      • Simple linear regression analysis.
      • Determining the line of best fit.
      • Understanding residuals and the coefficient of determination (R^2).
    • Multivariate Data Analysis
      • Introduction to multivariate statistics.
      • Multiple regression analysis.
      • Factor analysis and principle component analysis (PCA).
    • Experimental Design
      • Basics of experimental design and analysis.
      • Randomized block design.
      • Factorial experiments.

  • Advanced Level

    • Advanced Regression Techniques
      • Multiple linear regression with interaction terms.
      • Logistic regression for binary outcomes.
      • Generalized linear models.
      • Model selection and validation techniques.
      • Diagnostics for checking model assumptions.
    • Time Series Analysis
      • Components of time series data: trend, seasonality, and noise.
      • Moving averages and exponential smoothing.
      • Autoregressive (AR) and moving average (MA) models.
      • ARIMA and seasonal decomposition models.
    • Bayesian Statistics
      • Probability and prior distributions.
      • Bayes' Theorem application in hypothesis testing.
      • Bayesian inference and decision making.
      • Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC) simulations.
    • Advanced Topics in Multivariate Analysis
      • Cluster analysis.
      • Discriminant analysis.
      • Canonical correlation.
      • Structural equation modeling.
    • Big Data Analytics
      • Introduction to Big Data concepts.
      • Data mining techniques and algorithms.
      • Introduction to machine learning.
      • Use of statistical software for handling large datasets (e.g., R, Python, SAS).
    • Reporting and Communication of Results
      • Effective data visualization techniques for complex models.
      • Writing reports and interpreting results for different audiences.
      • Ethical considerations in data analysis.
