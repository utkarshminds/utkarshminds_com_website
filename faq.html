Frequently asked questions

Click the question below:

1. What courses do you offer?

We offer a wide range of courses in software development, including programming languages (Java, Python, C#), web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), mobile app development (Android, iOS), data science, and more. Check our course catalog for a complete list.

2. Who is the target audience?

Class 6-12, Under-graduates, graduates, post-graduates, doctoral students, home-makers, job seekers, senior citizens.

3. What is the duration of the training programs?

The duration varies depending on the course. We offer flexible scheduling options, including short-term intensive courses and long-term comprehensive programs. Check the specific course details for information on duration.

4. Is there any prerequisite for enrolling in your courses?

Prerequisites vary for each course. While some programs are suitable for beginners, others may require a basic understanding of specific programming languages or concepts. Please refer to the course descriptions for detailed prerequisites.

5. Do you provide placement assistance after completing the training?

Yes, we offer placement assistance to our students. Our dedicated placement cell works closely with industry partners to connect qualified candidates with job opportunities. We also conduct career development workshops to enhance employability skills.

6. Do you provide degree or diploma?

We provide only certification courses.

7. How can I enroll in a course?

You can contact us +91 961-999-77-97 on or write an email to pranav.nerurkar@utkarshminds.com and we will be in touch with you

8. Do you provide online training?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online training options to accommodate different learning preferences and geographic locations. Our online courses provide the same quality content and interaction with instructors as our in-person sessions.

9. What type of certifications do you provide?

Upon successful completion of our courses, participants receive a certificate of completion. The certificate is a testament to your skills and knowledge gained during the training and can be a valuable asset for your career.

10. Are there any discounts or scholarships available?

We periodically offer discounts and scholarships on our courses. Check our website or contact our admissions team for information on current promotions and eligibility criteria for scholarships.

11. Can I get a demo class before enrolling?

Yes, we often conduct demo classes or provide sample course materials for interested individuals. Contact our admissions team to schedule a demo or to inquire about accessing sample content.

12. How experienced are your instructors?

Our instructors are highly experienced professionals with extensive industry knowledge. They are experts in their respective fields and are dedicated to providing practical insights and real-world examples during the training sessions.
